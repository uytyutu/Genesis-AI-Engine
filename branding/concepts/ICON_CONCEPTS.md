# Signature Icon — Concept Comparison

## Brief

Redesign from scratch. **No plain letter V.** The V must live in **negative space** — a second-discovery moment.

---

## Concept A — **Confluence** (SELECTED)

**Symbol:** Two sculpted intelligence streams flow inward and converge on a luminous core. The gap between the streams forms a hidden **V**.

| Strength | Why |
|----------|-----|
| Hidden V | Negative space between twin ribbons — not a drawn letter |
| Scalability | Solid filled shapes read at 16px; core dot anchors recognition |
| Meaning | Many systems → one core (Virtus Core architecture) |
| Distinction | Unlike OpenAI blossom, Anthropic arcs, or Copilot loops |
| Premium | Sculpted curves, soft mass, restrained accent |

**Weakness:** Requires balance so streams don't read as "wings" at first glance — solved by downward convergence + core.

---

## Concept B — Meridian Fold

**Symbol:** Single continuous folded ribbon with void cutout.

| Strength | Why |
|----------|-----|
| Unity | One path = one system |
| Hidden V | Central void |

**Weakness:** Complex silhouette; inner void can muddy at 16–24px. Feels more "badge template" than flagship.

---

## Concept C — Nexus Orbit

**Symbol:** Orbital ring + twin arcs + central core.

| Strength | Why |
|----------|-----|
| Architecture | Suggests platform + satellites |

**Weakness:** Ring reads generic; thin accent strokes vanish at small sizes. Too close to "tech startup orbital" cliché.

---

## Decision

**Concept A — Confluence** → production master at `branding/vector/vector-app-icon.svg`.

Rationale: best balance of **instant recognition**, **hidden V discovery**, **16px legibility**, and **distinctiveness** from major AI marks.
