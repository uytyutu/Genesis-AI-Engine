## Virtus Core

**Virtus Core** is the technology platform for running a digital business.

It unites products, data, licenses, users, and automation in one ecosystem.

The customer does not buy a disconnected tool.  
They join a **platform that grows with their business**.

### What Virtus Core provides

- Website  
- Identity  
- Portal  
- Products  
- CRM  
- Analytics  
- Automation  
- Commercial Platform  
- AI Infrastructure  

### Client-facing signature

> **Powered by Virtus Core.**

---

## Vector

**Vector** is the face of the platform.

Not “just a chatbot.”  
Not “just an AI.”

**Vector** is the company’s **AI Business Employee** — understands the business, knows its processes, and talks to customers across channels.

### Positioning

> **Meet Vector.**  
> Your AI Business Employee.  
> Powered by Virtus Core.

### Role

Vector is the main interface between people and the platform.

People talk to **Vector**, not to technical modules:

- “Ask Vector.”  
- “Vector replied to the customer.”  
- “Vector took the request.”  
- “Vector booked the appointment.”  
- “Vector found it in the knowledge base.”  
- “Vector created a CRM task.”

Vector uses Virtus Core capabilities.  
Vector remains a **separate, memorable user brand**.

## Why this architecture

### For the customer

No need to understand internal modules.  
Their digital employee is simply named **Vector**.

### For marketing

A recognizable character for demos, ads, and stories — without renaming the platform every release.

### For scale

Virtus Core stays the stable platform brand.  
Vector gains new abilities without repositioning the whole stack.

---

## Vision

**Virtus Core** — the platform.  
**Vector** — the intelligence and primary conversation interface of that platform.

A modern digital ecosystem: the business runs infrastructure on Virtus Core and works day-to-day with its AI employee — **Vector**.
